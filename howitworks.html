Prep Co. offers a revolutionary buffet dining experience.

Here's how it works:

  1. Come on in and grab a tablet if you want to track what you're eating. Each tablet is linked to the scale next to each buffet item.
  2. Snag a tray if you're going to have many plates to juggle —we guarantee you will!
  3. When you find something tasty, put the paper serving dish on the corresponsing scale and weigh out your food. The calories and
    macros will display on your tablet as the weight changes!
  4. When you get to the amount of food you want, hit the "ok" button on the tablet. Viola! The stats are added to your meal.
  5. Get as much food as you want and watch how your stats change without all the calculating work on your end.
  6. Eat up!
